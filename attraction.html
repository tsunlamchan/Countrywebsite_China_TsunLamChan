Attractions

China has many popular tourist attractions that show both its natural beauty and modern lifestyle. Visitors can explore famous parks like the Summer Palace and Zhangjiajie National Forest Park, enjoy street food such as dumplings, noodles, and bao buns, or walk through busy areas like Shanghai’s Nanjing Road. China’s attractions offer a mix of nature, history, and culture, giving travelers many exciting places to see and experience.
