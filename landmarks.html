Famous Landmarks in China

















China is home to many famous landmarks that show its long history and unique culture. Some of the most well-known places include the Great Wall of China, the Forbidden City in Beijing, the Terracotta Army in Xi’an, and the modern Shanghai skyline with the Oriental Pearl Tower. These landmarks represent ancient achievements, powerful dynasties, and China’s rapid growth today. Each site has its own story and helps visitors understand China’s past and present.
